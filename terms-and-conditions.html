NSS KMIT Volunteer Application - Terms and Conditions

By submitting the application form, you agree to abide by the following rules and regulations as an NSS KMIT volunteer:

1. Eligibility Criteria

Only students of Keshav Memorial Institute of Technology (KMIT) are eligible to apply.

2. Accurate Information Required

All details submitted in the application form must be true and accurate. Providing false information will result in disqualification.

3. Photo Guidelines

  • Upload a passport-size photo with proper resolution.
  • Ensure the photo is official and appropriate.
  • Inappropriate photos will lead to rejection of the application.

4. Respect Deadlines

Submit the form within the specified timeline to be considered for selection.

5. Adherence to Rules

Read all the rules carefully before submitting the form. Any violation of these rules will result in immediate removal from the NSS KMIT group.

6. One Application Per Student

Ensure you fill out and submit the form only once. Duplicate submissions will not be considered.

7. Follow the Submission Process

After completing the application form:

  • Submit the redirected Google Form.
  • Join the WhatsApp group through the link provided to stay connected.

8. Active Participation

Volunteers must commit to participating in NSS activities with dedication and sincerity.

9. Event Permissions

Permissions granted for NSS activities are strictly for those activities only. Misusing them for personal purposes is prohibited.

10. Communication and Behavior

Maintain respectful communication with all NSS members and uphold the values of the NSS community.

By submitting the form, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to comply with these terms and conditions.
