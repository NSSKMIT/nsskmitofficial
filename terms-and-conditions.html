National Service Scheme (NSS) Coordinator Undertaking Form

I hereby affirm my commitment to the terms and conditions set forth by the National Service Scheme unit of KMIT. I understand that these rules are vital for upholding the integrity and effective functioning of the NSS team.

Compliance with Rules

I hereby commit to abiding by all college and council rules. I acknowledge that any violation of these rules may result in immediate termination from my position, without prior notice.

Attendance Requirement

I pledge to maintain a minimum attendance of 65%. Should I fail to meet this requirement, I will be granted a 40-day period to rectify my attendance and comply with the required standards.

Proper Use of Permissions

I recognize that any permissions granted by NSS for event participation are strictly designated for those activities only. I understand that utilizing this time to neglect classes or any academic commitments is strictly prohibited.

Team Harmony

I agree to foster a positive and harmonious environment within the NSS team. I understand that participation in conflicts or disputes undermines our collective mission, and I commit to maintaining a respectful and cooperative attitude towards my peers.

I confirm that I have read, understood, and fully agree to comply with these guidelines. I am aware that any failure to adhere to these terms may result in disciplinary action, including the possibility of termination from my role as NSS Coordinator.
